Lemon Broccoli Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250g of Pasta
  • 2 Lemons
  • 1 Large Head of Broccoli
  • Antipasti Mushroom jar
  • Capers

Method

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Add Broccoli to boiling pasta
  3. Squeeze Lemon juice into container
  4. Mix lemon juice and water from Pasta/ Broccoli boil in a container
  5. Add Capers and Mushrooms
  6. Enjoy!
