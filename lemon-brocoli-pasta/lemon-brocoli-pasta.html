Lemon Brocoli Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250g of Pasta
  • 2 Lemons
  • 1 Large Head of Brocoli
  • Antipasti Mushrooms
  • Capers
  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Add Brocoli to boiling pasta
  3. Squeeze Lemon juice into container
  4. Mix lemon juice and water from Pasta/ Brocoli boil in a container
  5. Add Capers and Mushrooms
  6. Enjoy!
